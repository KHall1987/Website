Welcome

Welcome to my professional site. I'm Kelvin Hall, a senior Instrumentation Electrical and Controls engineer with over a decade of experience building, integrating, and optimizing industrial automation systems. I created this site to support job applications and professional outreach.

My background includes extensive work in electrical design for industrial operations, particularly in the natural resource sector. I've led development and implementation efforts for automation systems used in high-reliability environments. I bring hands-on expertise in mechatronics, including motors, actuators, and sensor systems, and have operated as a system engineer on multifaceted, cross-disciplinary projects.

I have experience drafting electrical schematics, control narratives, and technical documentation, and have worked with PLC platforms including ABB, Allen-Bradley, Codesys, Schnieder, and Siemens. My work is grounded in real-world industrial needs—clear, robust, and safety-conscious.
